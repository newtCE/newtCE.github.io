Christopher Ekins is co-founder, art director and developer at game studio Newt Industries LLC.

Christopher has worked on multiple game titles in numerous capacities including in house and commissioned work. A strong foundation in classical art and multiple creative disciplines makes Christopher able to function as a bridge between art and engineering departments.

Currently working on a new large scope game project and engaging in ongoing education and training to further hone his abilities.
